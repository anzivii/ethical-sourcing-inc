Welcome to VerityScore

Search for a company to see its sector and ethical percentage rating.

Ratings are based on analysis of the company's Human Rights Due Diligence (HRDD) tier, Child/Forced Labor Risk, and sustainability. The ratings are out of 100, with higher scores indicating better ethical practices.

Ethical Ratings Breakdown:

80–100 → Very Ethical

60–79 → Ethical

40–59 → Moderately Ethical

Below 40 → Unethical

Additional Information Breakdown

HRDD Tier

Leader = top performers in CHRB / strong policies + enforcement

Strong = good systems, decent transparency

Moderate = partial systems or inconsistent disclosure

Weak = poor transparency or known gaps

Child/Forced Labor Risk

Low = minimal risk based on industry and disclosures

Medium = some risk factors present, but mitigated

High = significant risk factors with limited mitigation

Sustainability

High = strong environmental policies, renewable energy use, waste reduction

Medium = some sustainability initiatives, but room for improvement

Low = limited sustainability efforts or negative environmental impact

Notes:

Finance companies are low risk cause they have fewer physical supply chains

For unethical companies and those ratings lower than 40, there are some more ethical alternatives provided.
